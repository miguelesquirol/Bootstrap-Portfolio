CSJR

The CSJR (Centre for Services in Restorative justice) is the bilingual site for a non-profit project that relies on encounters between persons who directly or indirectly, have suffered or committed crimes of a similar nature in order to restore together the damage done by the crime.

I worked on this project, replacing the original site they had. From design (together with a graphic designer), to hosting and maintenance, the site is built on Wordpress, with security and multilingual plugins.
